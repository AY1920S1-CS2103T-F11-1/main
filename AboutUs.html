About Us

AddressBook3 (AB3) was developed by the se-edu team.
We morphed the project into Alfred - an app that is to be used by Hackathon HR managers to manage and organise Hackathons. This project was developed by group F11 of CS2103T. Our team repo can be found here.

We are a team based in the School of Computing, National University of Singapore.
